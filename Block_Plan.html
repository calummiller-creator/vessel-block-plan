Vessel Block Plan — Guide
Toolbar
Simops Highlights overlapping campaigns on the same asset with red shading. Campaigns always show on separate rows.
Vessel summaries Shows vessel utilisation bars at the top of each region. Click a bar for details.
On-location only Shows only Work segments for campaigns, with dashed gaps between them.
Expand L2 Globally expands all campaign sub-activities as inline rows.
Show fields Breaks assets with multiple fields into separate rows.
Show completed Shows activities with Completed status.
Interacting with bars
Click a bar Standalone activities show a floating detail popup. Campaign bars open a slide-in panel with stats, mini Gantt, and sub-activity details.
Expand button ▸ To the right of campaign bars — click to expand that campaign's L2 rows inline. Click again to collapse.
View campaign detail In the popup for a sub-activity, click this button to open the full campaign panel.
Bar styles
Solid bar Confirmed/Contracted or Ongoing
Hatched bar Awaiting confirmation/Contract
Desaturated + dashed Completed
Faded dashed outline Potential window (behind the main bar)
Text beside bar When a bar is too narrow, the label appears to the right
Grouping colours
Timeline
Auto-zoom The visible months adjust to fit the data. Narrow the date slicer and the timeline zooms in.
Today line Red vertical line marking today's date.
Region banner Dark header showing the current region (filtered by Power BI slicer).
Area headers Group assets within the region.
Power BI slicers
All slicers on the page (Region, Year, Date, Area, Asset, Vessel, Grouping, SPA, etc.) automatically filter this visual. The date slicer uses overlap logic — it shows any activity active during the selected range.
