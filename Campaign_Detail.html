Campaign Schedule — Guide

Campaign selector — click campaign chips at the top to toggle them. Use "Select all" or "Clear" to bulk select. Each chip shows the grouping colour.

Campaign cards — each selected campaign shows as a card with header info, stats breakdown (duration, on-location, port, transit days), a proportional Gantt chart, and an activity list.

Gantt chart — auto-scales to the campaign's date range. Month headers and week markers show the timeline. Bars are colour-coded by grouping with SubType patterns (hatching for port, faded for transit).

Power BI slicers — all slicers on the report page filter the available campaigns.

Print / present — use Ctrl+P to print. The toolbar and selector are hidden in print mode for clean output.
