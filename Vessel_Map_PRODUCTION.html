Vessel Positions Map — Guide
Map markers
Coloured circles Campaign/activity locations. Colour matches the grouping (Drilling = orange, IRM = blue, etc.). Click for details.
Small grey dots Field/asset locations with faint name labels. Toggle with the Fields button.
Numbered circles Area markers showing average position of all fields in an area, with field count. Toggle with Areas (mutually exclusive with Fields).
Orange squares Port locations. Toggle with the Ports button.
Clicking activities
Click a marker Shows a detail popup with vessel, grouping, status, SPA, organisation, dates, and position.
View campaign detail For activities in a campaign, click this button in the popup to open a panel with a mini Gantt chart, numbered activity list, and route map with sequence markers.
Click a table row Shows the detail popup and zooms the map to that location.
Select area tool
Select area Click the toggle, then drag a rectangle on the map. The table filters to campaigns within the box and other markers fade. Click the toggle again to clear.
Distance calculator
Toggle on Shows the calculator below the table. Select From and To from grouped dropdowns (ports and fields). Click Calculate for distance in NM and km, bearing, and a route line on the map.
Activity table
Grouped by field Activities sorted by field then date. Shows Activity, Vessel, SPA, Scope, From, and To. Click any row for details.
Power BI slicers
All slicers on the page automatically filter the map, table, and distance calculator options.
